Algorithm Deep-Dive

🧠

Cross-Modal Attention

Query features attend over image spatial tokens (16 patches): Attn(Q=query, K=V=img_tokens). Enables genuine modality interaction vs naive concatenation. Inspired by CLIP & Flamingo.

📊

MC-Dropout Uncertainty

T=50 stochastic forward passes with dropout ON ≈ sampling from approximate Bayesian posterior (Gal & Ghahramani, 2016). Separates epistemic (model) from aleatoric (data) uncertainty.

🎯

Adaptive Quiz (NP-Hard)

Optimal question ordering = NP-complete Optimal Decision Tree (Hyafil & Rivest, 1976). Our greedy mutual information maximization achieves (1−1/e) ≈ 63% approximation. Stops when P(dosha) > 82%.

🔥

GradCAM Saliency

αk = GlobalAvgPool(∂yc/∂Ak). Maps gradient × activation to spatial heatmap on the face image, showing which regions drove the dosha classification (Selvaraju et al., 2017).
